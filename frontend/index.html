VS.

Player 1 controls the white circles, while Player 2 controls the two pink circles. The larger circles represent each respective player's body, while their smaller circles are known as DEATH CIRCLES. Each player needs to control their death circle in an attempt to make contact with the other player's body while keeping their own body safe from the enemy's death circle.

Player 1 Death Circle Controls: W = up, A = left, S = down, D = right
Player 1 Body Controls: Y = up, G = left, H = down, J = right

Player 2 Death Circle Controls: ArrowUp = up, ArrowLeft = left, ArrowDown = down, ArrowRight = right
Player 2 Body Controls: Num8 = up, Num4 = left, Num5 = down, Num6 = right
